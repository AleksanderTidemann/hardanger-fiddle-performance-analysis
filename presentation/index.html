Exploring Hardanger Fiddle Performance Patterns Through Interactive Computational Tools

Introduction

  • Hardanger Fiddle Music
    • Associated with springar dance tradition.
    • Triplet meter with complex ornamentations and asymmetrical timing patterns.
      •
  • Irregular beat patterns are intimately related to aspects of motivic structures
    • Suggests that asymmetrical timing variations are related to motivic position rather than their beat position.
      •

Research Question

How can we design interactive computational tools to explore performance patterns in Hardanger fiddle music?

Approach

Develop a 3 interactive computational tools that explore timing patterns in Hardanger fiddle performances, focusing on the melodic-rhythmic structures

Toolkit design

  • Interactivity and engagement
  • A structural perspective
  • Multi-dimensional analysis
