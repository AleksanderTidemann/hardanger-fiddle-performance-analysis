Tool nr.1 and 2

1

Interactive Score

Goal

  • Data comprehension
  • Explore alternative performance timings.

Recieve data from MIRAGE and make sense of it in meaningful and interacive/engaging way

Through visually and interactive comparison. Gamification. Play game

Converts the data into a mix of piano roll and score representation

The green bars indicate beat and bar!! X-axis is time in ms.

Also, the ability to shift-adjust the score. avoid temporal compensation.

Ability to impose statistical beat duration distributions to ALL measures.

Explain more in-depth.

2

Timing patterns of repeating sections and phrases

Goal

  • Explore timing patterns from a structural perspective.
  • Increase data accessibility through visualization

first thing to notice is the dictionary. p data into more firendly format

second, in addition to perf-data, MIRAGE provided me with a list of all repeating sections and phrases. Result of structural analysis. list of numbers.

Choose a range, see if the particular range repeats itself in the peformance, plot timing patterns of all those repetitions.

Further Development

  • Propegate beat adjustments to all section and phrase repetitions.
  • Note scaling when adjusting beats.
  • Incorporate ornamentations
