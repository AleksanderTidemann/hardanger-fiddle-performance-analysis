Tool nr.1 and 2

1

Interactive Score

Goal

  • Data compreshension
  • Explore alternative performance timings.
  • Invite reflection on how players modify the written score during play.

Recieve data from MIRAGE and make sense of it in meaningful and interacive/engaging way

Through visually and interactive comparison. Gamification. Play game

Secondly, converted in score representation of the performance

virtue of ratios, enable making adjustments to the beats dynamically.

The way the notes are adjuted is not very much desirable. good for proto.

Also, the ability to shift-adjust the score. avoid temporal compensation.

Ability to impose statistical beat distributions is features.

So all of these features enable easy exploration and data gathering.

2

Timing patterns of motivic structures

Goal

  • Explore timing patterns from a structural perspective.
  • Increase data accessibility through visualization

first thing to notice is the dictionary. p data into more firendly format

second, patterns text. list of number which indicate where the motifs are in the score.

third, we select a range and plot the timing patterns.

instantly plot a desired range, see if the range conicides with any motivic strucutre, and plot the timing patterns of that structure.

Further Development

  • Propegate beat adjustments to all motivic instances.
